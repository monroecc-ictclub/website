Upcoming Events

The Information & Computer Technology Club hosts a variety of activities throughout the semester designed to help students learn, collaborate, and connect with others who share an interest in technology. Events may include club meetings, technical workshops, guest speakers, project nights, hands-on demonstrations, competitions, and other special activities. Whether you're looking to develop new technical skills or meet fellow technology enthusiasts, there's something for everyone.
