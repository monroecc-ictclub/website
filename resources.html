Learning Resources
ICT Learning Center

The Information & Computer Technologies (ICT) Learning Center is an excellent resource for students enrolled in Computer Science (CSC), Computer Programming & Technology (CPT), Information Technology (IT), and related courses.

Knowledgeable tutors, many of whom are fellow ICT students, are available to assist with coursework, programming assignments, technical concepts, and software used in ICT courses.

The Learning Center is also an excellent place to study, collaborate with classmates, prepare for exams, and meet other students pursuing technology-related degrees.

Location: Room 09-264
