Get Involved

The Information & Computer Technology Club is open to all Monroe Community College students, regardless of their major or level of technical experience.

Whether you're interested in programming, cybersecurity, networking, hardware, Linux, web development, or simply want to learn more about technology, you're welcome to join us.

Getting involved is easy. Attend one of our meetings or events, join our Discord community, or simply introduce yourself to one of the club officers. We'd be happy to help you get started.
